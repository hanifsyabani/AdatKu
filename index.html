    Benefits of Traditional House

    Traditional house preserves indigenous architectural heritage while embracing sustainable, low-carbon design principles.

    Cultural Identity

    Traditional houses reflect the cultural identity of a region or community. They often have distinctive architecture, ornaments, and patterns that are rich in cultural heritage. Preserving traditional houses can help conserve and pass on cultural heritage from generation to generation.

    Harmony with the Environment

    Traditional houses are often designed with a balance between human habitation and the surrounding environment. The building materials used are often sourced from sustainable local resources, such as wood, bamboo, and clay.

    Energy Efficiency and Sustainability

    Traditional houses often offer good energy efficiency. For instance, the use of natural materials like wood provides natural insulation properties, helping to maintain more stable indoor temperatures.
